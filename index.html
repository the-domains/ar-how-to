HP Augmented Reality Product Maintenance

Media Grand / YouTube
AR assisted product maintenance application for use with Epson Moverio smart-glasses. The apps provide step-by-step instructions to assist with basic maintenance procedures on a DL380P Gen8 Server. Utilising AR to overlay visual identifiers helps to immediately see where to place your hands, what to press, turn or pinch, or where to place a tool and the direction it needs to be turned or pushed.
